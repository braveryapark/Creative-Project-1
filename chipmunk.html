Wisconsin Chipmunk

Stuff about chipmunks.

  • There are hundreds of chipmunks, but populations are dropping.
  • They live mainly in Wisconsin and the Midwest Americas.
  • They are expected to survive one century before extinction.

What can be done?

  1. Legislation to limit rodent killing.
  2. Chipmunk sanctuaries.
  3. Funding for better protection of the species.
  4. Join a rally for animal rights.
