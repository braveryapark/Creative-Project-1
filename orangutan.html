Giant Orangutan

Stuff about orangutans.

  • There are 93 Giant Orangutans left in te world.
  • They live mainly in Asia and Australia.
  • They are endangered because of the price of their fur.
  • They are expected to survive two centuries before extinction.
  • They have distinctly orange fur.
  • They are usually very docile creatures until threatened.
  • They are herbivores, often eating wild fruit.
  • Their most famous theatrical role is The Jungle Book's King Louis.

What can be done?

  1. Legislation to limit or ban orangutan poaching.
  2. Orangutan sanctuaries.
  3. Funding for better protection of the species.
  4. Join a rally for animal rights.
